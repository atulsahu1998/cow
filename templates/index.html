About Us

Mission

" Cattle & Co.' mission is to provide shelter to the stranded cattle and best care. Extract the high quality raw materials to produce the highest-quality byproducts rich in their properties. We exist to attract and maintain cattle farmers and manufacturers of byproducts. Our aim is to create micro entrepreneurs. "

Objective

"Engage the farmers as part of this business, Provide safe shelters to cattle in need, Create a high quality raw material produce for a high quality byproduct, Reach the point of sustainable profitability, R&D to innovate byproducts from the raw materials available to expand the product line gradually, Educate the masses of the enormous amounts of benefits of our products "

Keys of our Success

"Product freshness and quality, Increase in number of Cattle farmers, Variety and price, Constant research of industry to keep knowledgeable of market needs, Management: products delivered on time, professional customer service, managing budgets, Constant evaluation of strengths and weaknesses with adjustments made as warranted."
